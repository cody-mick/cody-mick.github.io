Chamber Goals and Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce of the City of Rigby Idaho is designed to bring business together. Our main goal is to link together the businesses in the town into a single network in order to accurately represent the interests of the Chamber to local government officials.

The Chamber serves as a resource for local businesses to help them capitalize on continued growth in Jefferson County. Together, the Chamber, the local businesses, and the citizens of Rigby make our little city a great place to live, work, and play.
