Membership Tier
Non-Profit Tier: The Non-Profit Tier is designed for business that are just as the name implies, non-profit. Included in this tier is being listed in the Chamber Directory, and up 5 social media posts per month.

Bronze: The Bronze Tier is for small businesses looking to get ahead of the game. This includes everything from the nonprofit tier, plus an additional 5 social media posts per month, and a select number of tickets to business trainings put on by the Chamber.

Silver: The Silver Tier includes everything from the previous tiers, plus an additional 5 social media posts, giving a total of 15 posts per month, as well as an increased number of tickets to business trainings, luncheon tickets (when available), and a small advertisement space on our website.

Gold: The Gold Tier is the ultimate package. Everythin from the previous tiers is included, and again, with 5 extra social media posts. Bringing the total to 20/month. This tier also includes a premium advertisement spot on the chamber website, the most tickets to trainings and luncheons, and premium advertisement space at community and local events.
