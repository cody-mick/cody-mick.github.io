Summer-Long Event Hosted by the City of Preston

By Cody Mickelsen, Alpha Forecast News

Preson Pancakes in the Park Initiative

During the entirity of the summer months, the City of Preston is hosting a pancakes in the park town get together! Every single saturday morning, come and join us for breakfast, games, socialization, and more! There are plenty of things to do!

Come and meet the members of City Council, bring your ideas and comments to us! View local attractions, and meet people! We know that it has been a hard year for everyone, but with restrictions lifting and more and more activities becoming safe to do, this is the perfect opportunity to meet your neighbors, and get to know the town. Every one is welcome! Bring you families, friends, and even strangers!

The City of Preston hopes that these events will be able to bring people together, and also create a warm and inviting atmosphere for locals and visitors alike. We have a fine selection of breakfast foods, with the staple being the pancakes! (gluten-free options available.) There is also local attractions as the even takes place in the central park. Come and enjoy breakfast and learn about the history of Preston.

We can't wait to see you there!
