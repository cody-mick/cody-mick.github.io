Site Purpose

The purpose of Alpha Forecast is for people of all demograhics to be able to come to the site, find the weather they are looking for, see suggestions about what can be done during the current weather conditions, and showcase a number of images and such in a gallery for people to view at their leisure. The site is designed to inform people about the weather, local events, and other things, as well as be easy to navigate and find the information you are looking for.
